The Colors Of The Rainbow ( R O Y G B I V )

R ed is the color at the long wavelength end of the visible spectrum of light, next to orange and opposite violet.

O range is the colour between yellow and red on the spectrum of visible light.

Y ellow is the color between green and orange on the spectrum of light.

G reen is the color between cyan and yellow on the visible spectrum.

B lue is the colour of light between violet and cyan on the visible spectrum.

I ndigo is a deep color close to the color wheel blue as well as to some variants of ultramarine.

V iolet is the color of light at the short wavelength end of the visible spectrum, between blue and invisible ultraviolet.
